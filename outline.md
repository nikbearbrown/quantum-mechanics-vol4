## Chapters

1. **Mixed States and the Density Matrix** — Distinguish pure from mixed; compute reduced density matrices.
2. **Composite Systems and Entanglement** — Identify entanglement; compute the Schmidt decomposition.
3. **Bell's Theorem and the CHSH Experiment** — Compute the CHSH bound and interpret the experimental violation.
4. **Quantum Gates, Circuits, and Algorithms** — Build a small circuit and simulate its output.
5. **Quantum Teleportation and Dense Coding** — Trace a teleportation protocol step by step.
6. **Open Systems: Decoherence and the Lindblad Equation** — Simulate decoherence with a Lindblad/Bloch model.
7. **Measurement, Interpretations, and the Quantum–Classical Boundary** — Compare interpretations against what experiment constrains.
8. **Quantum Hardware: Qubits, NV Centers, and the NISQ Era** — Map a physical platform onto the formalism.
9. **Error, Noise, and the Threshold Theorem** — Explain why fault tolerance is possible in principle.
10. **Capstone — Quantum Mechanics in Research** — Read and reconstruct a current paper's core result.
